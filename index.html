Cloud Computing provides the speed, scalability, and flexibility that enables businesses to develop, innovate, and support business IT solutions. Cloud Computing allows you to rent instead of buy your IT. Rather than investing heavily in databases, software, and hardware, companies opt to access their compute power via the internet, or the cloud, and pay for it as they use it. These cloud services now include, but are not limited to, servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, and business intelligence.
